Welcome to Cole's About Me Page

Loved the numbers and mental work. It didn't quite suit me a hundred percent though. So here I am again, starting another direction.

Little details about me. I love tattoos and piercings. I love art, particularly the darker sides of it. I love tea and coffee. I love a great IPA, the hoppier the better. I also enjoy alcohol, but not generally not mixed. On the rocks usually or served in a way that I can slowly enjoy it. I enjoy a great imaginative book while curled up on the couch with a dog cuddled next to me or in my lap.
